Bundt Cake

Kuglóf is a traditional Central European yeast cake, baked in its signature ridged mold. Soft, airy, and lightly sweet, it’s often flavored with raisins, nuts, or a dusting of powdered sugar. Simple yet elegant, this classic cake is perfect for breakfast, afternoon coffee, or as a festive treat to share.

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 100 ml milk
  • 30 g butter
  • Pinch of salt

Process

  1. Warm the milk and dissolve the yeast (if using).
  2. Mix flour, sugar and salt in a bowl; add egg and milk.
  3. Knead until smooth, then fold in melted butter.
  4. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  5. Pour into a greased Bundt pan and bake until golden.
  6. Cool, dust with powdered sugar, and serve.
