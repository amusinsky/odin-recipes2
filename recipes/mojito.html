Mojito

The Mojito is a refreshing Cuban cocktail that perfectly balances sweetness, citrus, and minty freshness. Light and invigorating, it’s a classic summer drink that’s as easy to make as it is to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 60 ml white rum
  • 120 ml soda water
  • Ice cubes

Process

  1. Place mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar in a glass.
  2. Muddle gently to release flavors.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour in the white rum.
  5. Top up with soda water and stir gently.
  6. Garnish with extra mint and a lime slice, then serve.
